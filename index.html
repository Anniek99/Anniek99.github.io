Annie Katz — Hands-On Engineering Portfolio

I build engineering projects in healthcare, usually based on real problems my family, friends, or I have experienced. Everything I make is meant to help someone in a real, practical way.

Grip Hero (v1)

A load-cell hand-strength trainer I built for my sister with dyspraxia when she struggled with existing therapy tools. This was my first experience designing a mechanical + sensing system, and it taught me how friction affects accuracy.

A reusable abdominal band that measures gut sounds and temperature overnight using three contact microphones and a thermistor. Built to study digestive rhythms and circadian timing.
